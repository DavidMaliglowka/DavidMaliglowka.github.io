Hey, I'm David

TL;DR I'm a designer, developer, and entrepreneur. Basically I find problems, solve them, and then build solutions to those problems.

In college I co-founded a company, Drops, which made it easy for people to donate their spare change to charity. We attracted awesome advisors like Sean Rad (Founder Tinder) and Jessica Jackley (Founder Kiva). We launched partnerships with amazing nonprofits including the United Nations Foundation and launched the app on the season finale of Apple's Planet of the Apps. It even got me featured in the Los Angeles Business Journal's 20 in their 20s.

This website is a work in progress. I'll be updating it in my freetime, but my goal is to make it a place to showcase all the ideas I play with.
